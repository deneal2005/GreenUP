## 2. MISSION, VISION & VALUES

### Mission
To empower students and communities to take measurable environmental action by making sustainability tracking transparent, verifiable, and rewarding.

### Vision
A world where environmental action is visible, celebrated, and accessible to everyone—from individual students to entire campuses and communities—creating a culture of accountability and collective impact.

### Core Values
- **Transparency**: All actions verified with GPS + photo proof on a public world map
- **Gamification**: Making environmental action rewarding through points, levels, badges, and social competition
- **Community**: Teams, leaderboards, and collective impact drive social motivation
- **Accessibility**: Works globally across 30+ currencies; mobile-first, no paywall
- **Simplicity**: Minimal dependencies, fast loading, works offline in demo mode
