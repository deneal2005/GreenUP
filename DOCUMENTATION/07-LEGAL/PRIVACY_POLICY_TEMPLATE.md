## PRIVACY POLICY

### 1. INTRODUCTION

GreenUP ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") operates the GreenUP platform, a web application for tracking environmental actions (tree planting, cleanups) with GPS verification and gamification.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

**Please read this Privacy Policy carefully.** If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Services.

---

### 2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

#### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

**Account Registration**:
- Email address
- Password (encrypted)
- Username (chosen or auto-generated)
- Full name (optional)
- College/Organization (optional)
- Country (from country dropdown)
- Bio (optional, max 200 chars)
- Profile avatar (image upload)

**Environmental Actions**:
- Photos (before/after images)
- GPS coordinates (latitude, longitude)
- Reverse-geocoded place names (generated from GPS)
- Tree species or cleanup waste types
- Weight estimates (for cleanup)
- Action notes/description
- Date and time of action

**Donations**:
- Donor name (or anonymous)
- Donation amount
- Currency

**Teams**:
- Team membership
- Team creation (if you create a team)

#### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Usage Information**:
- Pages visited
- Time spent on each page
- Links clicked
- Browser type and operating system
- IP address (used to detect country for currency selection)

**Cookies & Storage**:
- Session tokens (localStorage)
- User preferences (theme, last-selected team)
- Demo mode data (if using guest mode)

**Location Information**:
- GPS coordinates (only when you choose to log an action)
- City/country from reverse geocoding
- IP-based location (for currency detection)

### 6. YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS

### 6.1 GDPR Rights (if in EU)
- **Right to Access**: Request copy of your data
- **Right to Rectification**: Correct inaccurate data
- **Right to Erasure** ("Right to be Forgotten"): Delete your account and data
- **Right to Data Portability**: Download your data in machine-readable format
- **Right to Object**: Opt-out of certain processing
- **Right to Lodge Complaint**: File complaint with your supervisory authority

### 6.2 CCPA Rights (if in California)
- **Right to Know**: What data we collect and how we use it
- **Right to Delete**: Request deletion of your data
- **Right to Opt-Out**: Opt-out of data sales (we don't sell data)
- **Right to Non-Discrimination**: We won't discriminate for exercising rights

### 6.3 Other Rights
- Opt-out of email communications (future feature)
- Make profile private (future feature)

### 8. DATA SECURITY

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

- **Encryption in Transit**: HTTPS (TLS 1.2+) for all connections
- **Encryption at Rest**: Supabase PostgreSQL encryption
- **Access Control**: Row-Level Security (only see own data)
- **Backups**: Daily automated backups with encryption
- **No storage of payment card data**: Donations are currently simulated

**Limitation**: No security measure is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
