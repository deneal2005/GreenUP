Field manual

Proof of work. Literally.

No vague pledges, no carbon hand-waving. Three steps, all of them outdoors.

FIG. 01📸

Snap it

Plant a sapling or clear a patch of litter. For clean-ups, shoot a before and an after — the comparison is the proof.

FIG. 02🛰️

Pin it

GPS and timestamp lock your action to a real place and moment. It lands on the world map for everyone to see.

FIG. 03🌳

Grow

Earn points, unlock badges, climb from Seed to Forest Guardian. Your college competes for the Golden Sapling 🏆.
